RoMaDO-RA

Second workshop on Robotic Manipulation of Deformable Objects in Real-world Applications
on-line edition at IROS 2021 from September 27 to October 1, 2021.

Instructions for contributer papers

Contributed papers will be handled outside of Papercept. The important dates are as follows:
• Your contributions under the form of short (3+n) papers or regular (6+n) papers have to be submitted before August 13, 2021
• Papers will be reviewed and acceptance will be notified before August 27, 2021
• The final version of the paper and a video presentation will be requested al least 10 days before the workshop opening (duration of the video to be decided)
• Best papers will be advised to submit a full version for RA-L special issue on Robotic Handling of Deformable Objects

Hint to authors: RA-L editors will not accept precedently published reasearch. In the RA-L manuscript, definite improvements should be proposed compared to the workshop version.
