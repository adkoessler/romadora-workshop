RoMaDO-RA

Second workshop on Robotic Manipulation of Deformable Objects in Real-world Applications
on-line edition at IROS 2021 from September 27 to October 1, 2021.

  • Aug. 13 Contributed papers submission
  • Aug. 27 Contributed papers acceptance
  • Sept. 17 Final version + video

  • Short paper

    The easiest way to contribute is to send us a short paper. The format is (3+n), that is a limit of 3 pages for the body of the text, excluding references.

  • Regular paper

    Do you have a paper that you can already present? Send it to us with a limit of 7 pages for the whole article.

  • RA-L special issue

    Best papers will be advised to submit a full version for RA-L special issue on Robotic Handling of Deformable Objects

Special instructions

  • Contributions will be handled outside of the conference system. Send them to the main contact: Adrien.Koessler[AT]sigma-clermont[DOT]fr
  • General advice applies regarding the quality of the paper: originality, strength of the contribution, logical progression and quality of writing are the main criteria.
  • If you submit an extended version of your paper to RA-L special issue, definite improvements should be proposed compared to the workshop version.
